How To Play

You are a dinosaur who loves to run! Run as far as you can while avoiding obstacles. If you hit one, its Game Over. Using the up arrow key, you can either click it once to jump over small obstacles, or click it twice to double jump over larger ones. The farther you can make it, the higher the score! You can even see the top ten highest scores by pressing the scoreboard button on the home page. Happy Jumping!
